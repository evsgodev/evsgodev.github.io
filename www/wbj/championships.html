Расписание

Martches may start 30 minnutes in advance. Athletes need to be ready to compete inside the warm-up area at lest 40 minutes prior to the scheduled time.

All divisions (except Black belt adult) must register in the Open Class within 15 min after receiving their medal award. After that, registration will be closed.

All athlete registered in the open class division are required to report to the warm up area for check in, 40 minutes prior to the schedule.
